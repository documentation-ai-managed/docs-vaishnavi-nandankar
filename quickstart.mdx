## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have the following ready:

<Callout kind="info">

- A valid email address for account verification
- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- Optional: Node.js `{^18.0.0}` if using the CLI for uploads

</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Sign up for Vaishnavi Nandankar in under a minute.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Signup Page" icon="external-link">
    Navigate to `https://vaishnavinandankar.com/signup` in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, create a strong password, and complete the CAPTCHA.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for the verification link and click it to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to `https://vaishnavinandankar.com/login` and sign in with your credentials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create Your First Documentation Space

Once logged in, set up a space for your project.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click New Space" icon="plus">
    From the dashboard, select the `New Space` button in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name Your Space" icon="edit-3">
    Enter a name like `My Project Docs` and add an optional description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Template" icon="file-text">
    Select `Blank Space` for a clean start or pick a template for quick setup.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create and Enter" icon="check-circle">
    Click `Create`. You'll land in your new space's editor.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Upload Initial Project Files

Add your project files to the space via web or CLI.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web Drag & Drop" icon="upload">
    In your space, open the `Files` tab. Drag files like `README.mdx` or `api.mdx` directly into the upload zone.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="CLI Upload" icon="terminal">
    Install the CLI first:

    <CodeGroup tabs="npm,yarn">
    ````bash
    npm install -g @vaishnavinandankar/cli
    ````

    ````bash
    yarn global add @vaishnavinandankar/cli
    ````
    </CodeGroup>

    Authenticate and upload:

    ````bash
    vn login
    vn space select "My Project Docs"
    vn upload ./src/ --include="*.mdx,*.json"
    ````
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">

After uploading, refresh your space to see files in the sidebar. Start editing `index.mdx` for your homepage.

</Callout>
