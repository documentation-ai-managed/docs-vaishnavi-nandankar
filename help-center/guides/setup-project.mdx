## Overview

Create a new project space for your docs.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Projects">
    ````
    From the dashboard, click "Projects" on the sidebar.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create new">
    ````
    Click "New Project" and enter a name.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finish setup">
    ````
    Choose visibility and invite teammates as needed.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
