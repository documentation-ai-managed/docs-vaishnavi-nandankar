## Overview

Projects are organized in your dashboard. Access them anytime after login.

## Steps

1. Sign in at `dashboard.vaishnavinandankar.com`
2. Click on "Projects" in the sidebar.
3. Select your desired project from the list.

## Tip

Use the search bar to quickly find a project by name.
