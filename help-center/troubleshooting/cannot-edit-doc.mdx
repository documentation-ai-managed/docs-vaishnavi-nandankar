---
title: Cannot Edit Documentation
description: Quick fix if you can't edit documentation pages.
---

## Symptoms

- Edit button is disabled
- Error: "Permission denied"

## Causes

- You're not logged in
- No edit permissions

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check login status">
    ````
    Ensure you are signed in to your account.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request permissions">
    ````
    Contact the project owner to grant you 'Editor' access.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
